WHAT MAKES POSHN

Poshn is a brand launched by the company Fettle Works LLP. The word 'Fettle' means to be fit, and thereby, the goal of the company has always been to make cool and fun products, that not only help in health and fitness, but also make no sacrifices on taste whatsoever.

WHAT MAKES POSHN

"With Posh. we have strived to create a yummy functional juice, that is low on calories, has no added sugar, and completely natural, just to give our customers a delicious, guilt free, healthy and nutritious solution to their daily needs." - Rahul Kakkad
We look forward to launching more and more products that will change the way we look at healthy products.

WHAT MAKES POSHN

We believe, we have created formula to help one energize when feeling low, relax when one needs it the most. With no sacrifices made on taste, thanks to the delicious fruits handpicked by us, we have made sure we deliver on the function by boosting these juices with herbs such as Green Tea, Ginseng and Chamomile, thereby creating a recipe that we like to truly call: #NaturesOwnFormula

WHAT MAKES POSHN

Fruits + Herbs = Function // Our Secret Potion
We Also further add vitamins and minerals along with Stevia for sweetness, which leaves us with a very nutritious, low calorie product, without any added sugar, which is how we came up with the hindi term: Poshn,
This eventually led us to creating our brand name:

SLAYING SINCE 2016, SEE HOW!

2016

We began the research.
– We started looking for the right ingredients that could help in either Energy or Relaxation
– We started looking for the right vendors and manufacturers to help us with the manufacturing process

2017

After Various types of trials and errors, we began SAMPLING!
– We personally stood at each and every stall
– We heard what the customers had to say and made tweaks based on that
– After a successful sampling of almost 6 months, we were ready to hit the market

2018

Hitting the market
– A large part of this year was spent in solving all the legalities and government formalities.
– All lab tests and shelf life studies were done and then we were ready to make our distribution tie ups
– By October, we had taken our first step into retail.
– By November, we had started selling online.

2019

The call of Victory
